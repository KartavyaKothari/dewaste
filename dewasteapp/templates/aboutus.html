About project

A course project with CTARA IIT bombay, under guide Prof. Sudhanshu Mallick

We the students of IIT bombay enrolled ourselves into a project based course as we strogly feel about the e waste situation in india. In our many field visits we found that there exists a strong disposal network. This is majorly run by the back economy and can be leveraged to a great extent for the upliftment of that economy while preventing some electronic waste to reach our oceans/landfills harming life in it.

Prof. Sudhanshu Mallick has played a vital role in guiding us to the correct path to proceed on. We also thank the bhangarwalas who helped in understanding the process and closely look at their methods of stacking, breaking and clustering e waste. We would also like to thank the CTARA department for the oppputunity to work on this. Last but not the least, we are greatful to people who gave their time in filling our surveys to help us understand what to build.
